Welcome! My name is Shuguang Chen. 

I am a 3rd-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the [University of Houston](https://www.uh.edu/). I work at [Research in Text Understanding and Analysis of Language (RiTUAL)](http://ritual.uh.edu/) lab, supervised by [Dr. Thamar Solorio](http://solorio.uh.edu/). My research interests lie in the field of Natural Language Processing, with a special focus on named entity recognition and linguistic code-switching.
